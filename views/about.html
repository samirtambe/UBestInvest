About

Our priary goal is to introduce investing to teenagers so that they may develop an interest in finance. Secondly, we want to enhance their overall financial acumen with respect to investing so they may benefit from it throughout their lives. We aim to give users a website where they can act like investors with real financial data and can simulate the the investment process.

Technology Stack

We are passionate about using the latest and greatest technologies to for the benefit and convenience of the user. (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript, AngularJS, UI-Router, Bootstrap, D3 Charts)
